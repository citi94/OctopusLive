Octopus Live - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 March 2026

What data we collect

Octopus Live stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your Octopus Energy API key
  • Your Octopus Energy account number
  • Your smart meter device identifier (discovered automatically)

How data is used

Your credentials are used solely to fetch your electricity usage data from the Octopus Energy API. All data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server other than Octopus Energy's own API (api.octopus.energy).

Data storage

Credentials and configuration are stored in your device's sandboxed app storage using iOS App Group UserDefaults. This data is encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection.

Data sharing

We do not collect, share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time using the "Delete All Data" button in the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Third-party services

The app communicates only with the Octopus Energy API. Your API key and account data are sent directly to Octopus Energy to authenticate and retrieve your usage data. Octopus Energy's own privacy policy governs their handling of your data.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us via the app's App Store listing.

Octopus Live is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Octopus Energy Ltd. Octopus Energy is a trade name of Octopus Energy Ltd.
